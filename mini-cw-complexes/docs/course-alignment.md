# Course Alignment: 9-School Curriculum Mapping

## MIT (18.901 Algebraic Topology)
- CW complexes: Chapter 0 of Hatcher
- Cellular homology: Chapter 2.2
- Euler characteristic: Chapter 2.2
- Cover: skeleton, subcomplex, CW pair, product, quotient

## Stanford (MATH 215A Algebraic Topology)
- Fundamental group via CW complexes
- Covering spaces as 1-dimensional CW complexes
- Homology via cellular chain complex

## Princeton (MAT 520 Algebraic Topology)
- CW approximation theorem
- Whitehead theorem
- Obstruction theory
- Spectral sequences via CW filtration

## Berkeley (MATH 215A Algebraic Topology)
- Homotopy extension property for CW pairs
- Cellular approximation
- Mayer-Vietoris for CW complexes

## Cambridge (Part III Algebraic Topology)
- Model categories and CW complexes
- Rational homotopy theory
- Spectra and stable homotopy

## Oxford (C3.1 Algebraic Topology)
- Category theory perspective on CW complexes
- Homotopy colimits and CW construction
- Simplicial sets vs CW complexes

## ETH (401-3462 Algebraic Topology)
- Serre spectral sequence via CW filtration
- Eilenberg-MacLane spaces
- Postnikov towers

## ENS (Topologie Algebrique)
- Bourbaki approach to CW complexes
- Cellular decompositions of homogeneous spaces
- Morse theory and CW structures

## Tsinghua/THU (Algebraic Topology)
- Fundamental computations: S^n, RP^n, CP^n
- Surfaces and their CW structures
- Applications to manifold topology
